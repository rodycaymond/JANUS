Create Your Future

The JANUS Program meets the unique needs of transitioning ARSOF by equipping our clients with the tools and information they need to find purpose and fulfillment in the next chapter of their lives.

Know Your Purpose

Understand what brings you true satisfaction with little to no sense of burden, regardless of the effort required and show up with enthusiasm and energy.



The JANUS Program helps you discover your purpose by:

  • Identifying Your Core Values
  • Identifying Your Strengths
  • Creating Accountability

Build a Meaningful Network

Create a personal community based on genuine mutual interest and benefit.



The Janus Program helps you build a meaningful network by:

  • Being Generous
  • Being Open
  • Being Human

Redefine Your Identity

Don't allow who you were to limit who you want to be. De-couple from your identity as a soldier.



The Janus Program helps you redefine your identity by:

  • Finding Your Tribes
  • Staying Humble
  • Keeping What Works and Discarding What Doesn't

Did You Know?

60% of veterans do not last more than one year in their first post service career because existing programs focus on finding a job instead of finding fulfillment.

You Are Not Alone

The JANUS Program is your personal gateway to a powerful network of vetted and validated resources and partners to help you transition with confidence.
